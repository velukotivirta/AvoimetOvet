Odin's bananaomelett



Banana pancake for the ages.

This pancake recipe has been given from friend to friend, parent to child in the south of Finland for the last 5 years.

Now it's delicious, thick, sweet zest is here for you to experience as well.

Ingredients

Base

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 sweet (ripe) bananas
  • 1 dl oats
  • Spoonfuls of olive oil for frying

Addatives

  • A splash of oatmilk
  • Kardemum
  • Blueberries
  • Peanut butter
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Make the "dough"

    2. Add the eggs, bananas, oats, oatmilk, kardemum and peanut butter to a container.

    Mash all the ingredients together with a masher.

  2. Fry the thing

    3. Add olive oil to the frying pan and fry it until solid.
